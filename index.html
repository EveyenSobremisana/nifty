Nifty is a fashion brand based in Vancouver, British Colombia. We collaborate and use a co-creation process with local talented indigenous artisans in Vancouver to fuse an ancient indigenous craft & art with a modern design approach to come up with unique designs.

Our collections offer fresh designs while supporting the culture of the indigenous tribes through the preservation of crafts, designs and ancestral wisdom. Each purchase of our jewelry, hats & other accessories funds one hour of training for a group of local artisans.
